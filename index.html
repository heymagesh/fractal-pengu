About Fractal Pengu

Fractal Pengu is a community driven meme cryptocurrency created on the Solana blockchain. Inspired by fractal mathematics and internet culture, the project celebrates creativity, decentralization and community driven innovation.

The penguin represents curiosity, exploration and resilience across the web3 ecosystem. Fractal Pengu is not just a token but a digital movement built by internet culture.

Tokenomics

The Fractal Pengu token has a fixed supply of 300 million tokens. 270 million tokens are circulating while 30 million tokens remain in vesting.
