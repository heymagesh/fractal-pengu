About Fractal Pengu

Fractal Pengu is a community driven meme cryptocurrency created on the Solana blockchain. The project draws inspiration from fractal mathematics where patterns repeat infinitely and evolve into complex structures over time. Just like fractals expand endlessly, the Fractal Pengu community represents continuous growth, creativity and collective innovation within the decentralized web3 ecosystem. The penguin mascot symbolizes curiosity, exploration and resilience. It represents the adventurous spirit of internet culture where memes, ideas and creativity spread across the blockchain at lightning speed. Fractal Pengu is not just a token but a digital movement where culture, technology and humor merge together.

Tokenomics

The tokenomics structure of Fractal Pengu is designed to support long term community participation and sustainable ecosystem development. The project has a fixed supply of 300 million tokens ensuring scarcity while maintaining sufficient liquidity for decentralized trading. Out of the total supply, 270 million tokens are circulating within the market, allowing the community to actively participate in trading and ecosystem growth. The remaining 30 million tokens are allocated for vesting to support future development, marketing initiatives and long term community expansion. Fractal Pengu is listed across decentralized exchanges including Orca and multiple Solana DEX platforms enabling easy access for traders and investors across the ecosystem.
